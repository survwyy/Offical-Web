Privacy Policy

OneTap is a desktop assistant for CS2 players. This page summarizes the privacy expectations for the official website and related support contact.

01
Website analytics

The website may record basic interaction events such as download, navigation, language, and contact clicks to understand product interest and improve the site.

02
Support email

If you contact support@onetap.cool, we use your message and email address only to respond to support, business, download, or media requests.

03
Desktop product data

Match demos, inventory details, hardware profiles, and generated highlight assets are handled by the desktop product workflow. Final product data processing rules should be confirmed in the released application.

04
Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@onetap.cool.
